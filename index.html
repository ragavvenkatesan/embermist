    Jennifer Li

    Lead Vocal

    Jennifer is the lead vocal for Ember Mist. Her music influences are Paramore, Nirvana, Avril Lavigne, and Olivia Rodrigo.

    Fun fact: She was originally the drummer for Ember Mist, before transitioning into a full-time singer.

    Shuai Han

    Guitar, Vocal

    Han is the lead guitarist and backing vocals for Ember Mist. His music influences are Slash, My chemical romance, The Beatles, and Dream Theater.

    Fun Fact: Han is the founder of the band and is responsible for bringing everyone together.

    Ragav Venkatesan

    Bass, Vocal

    Ragav is Ember Mist's bassist and backing vocalist. His music influences covers a wide range including Avial, Motherjane, AR Rahman, Snarky Puppy, Periphery, Iron Maiden, Alice in Chains and Aristocrats. He is also the producer and sound engineer for Ember Mist.

    Scott Langdon

    Drums

    Scott is Ember Mist's drummer. His music influences are Led Zeppelin, Alice in Chains, Soundgarden, Nirvana, The Beatles and Elton John. He is Seattle born and draws a lot of influences from local bands.

    Lucid Dream Music Festival, 2023

    Lucid dream Music Festival was Ember Mist's debut live performance.

    The setlist for this show consisted of three covers in the form of Still into you by Paramore, Unholy by Sam Smith, and Whatsername by Green Day. Alongside the covers, we also debuted four originals in the form of Don't you dare, Golden Era, Again, and Bloom.

    The video recording of Bloom from this performance is now available.
